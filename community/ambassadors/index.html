What does an Ambassador do?

  • Organize or conduct workshops, meetups, and other exciting events to introduce Ballerina to fellow developers.
  • Develop tutorials, blogs, and videos showcasing Ballerina's capabilities.
  • Engage with the Ballerina community to foster collaboration and knowledge sharing.
  • Provide feedback on developer experience with Ballerina.

Perks of becoming an Ambassador

Exclusive learning opportunities

Get access to exclusive learning resources and mentorship.

Professional development

Enhance your skills in public speaking, content writing, and contributing to open-source projects.

Rewards and recognition

Earn recognition with certificates, badges, swag, and many more exclusive rewards.

Community influence

Create content and organize events to inspire and engage your local developer community.
